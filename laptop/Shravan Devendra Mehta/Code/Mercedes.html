2019 Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG

The Mercedes-Benz G63 AMG is an iconic luxury SUV that combines off-road capability with high-performance AMG engineering. It features a bold design, a handcrafted engine, and cutting-edge technology.

Key Specs:


  • Engine: 4.0L Twin-Turbo V8
  • Horsepower: 577 HP
  • 0-60 mph: ~4.3 seconds
  • Top Speed: 149 mph
