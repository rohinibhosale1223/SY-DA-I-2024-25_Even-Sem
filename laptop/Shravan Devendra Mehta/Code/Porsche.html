Porsche 911

The Porsche 911 is a legendary sports car known for its performance, timeless design, and engineering excellence. It features a rear-engine layout, precise handling, and a powerful flat-six engine.

Key Specs:


  • Engine: 3.0L Twin-Turbo Flat-Six
  • Horsepower: 379-640 HP
  • 0-60 mph: ~3.0 seconds
  • Top Speed: 205 mph
